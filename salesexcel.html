About Project

The growth of supermarkets in most populated cities are increasing and market competitions are also high. This dataset is one of the historical sales of supermarket company.

The purpose of this project is to analyze how different products are purchased by different customers.

Data

Key takeaways

  • Sales in consumer segment are higher than corporate and home office altoghether.
  • Asia pacific region covers the market by 31% in total sales.
  • The total sales from 2012 to 2026 are around 4.9 million dollars.
  • On an average, the delievery days are 4.
